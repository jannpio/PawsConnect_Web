About Us

At PawsConnect, we believe that every pet deserves exceptional care and a loving connection. As a dedicated veterinary clinic and pet shop, we provide a full range of services—from medical consultations and vaccinations to grooming, training, and wellness plans—all under one roof.

Our team is made up of compassionate professionals who treat every pet like family. Whether it’s a routine checkup or a special procedure, we’re here to support your pet’s health and happiness at every stage of life. And beyond medical care, we offer carefully selected products to meet your pet’s everyday needs—from nutrition to playtime.
